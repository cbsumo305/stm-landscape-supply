Where quality meets reliability.

We are Mobile's best choice for landscaping and lawn care supplies.

With our expert knowledge and extensive experience, you'll have the best yard on the block!

Adam & Melia Cornell

Owners

Adam Cornell, a native of Saraland, Alabama began STM Landscape Supply in 2017 with his wife, Melia Cornell. With Adam’s extensive landscaping experience, he had a specific vision for what STM Landscape Supply would offer. Coupled with Melia’s customer service expertise, they quickly made STM a staple for residents and landscapers in North Mobile. Adam and Melia currently live in Saraland, Alabama. They have a daughter, Adeline and a son on the way! They are active in their church, and they love spending time outdoors with their family.

Amber MacFarland-Wiese

General Manager

Amber joined the team in 2021, bringing her background in sales and business development to help STM grow and service Mobile and Baldwin County. She fell in love with the community and landscape supply business, and is always working hard to expand the business. Amber lives in Mobile with her husband Matt, and their son James. When she isn’t at the office or on the phone with customers, you can find her relaxing by the pool or listening to True Crime podcasts.

Emily Hanks

Sales Associate

Emily was born and raised in Mobile, AL. She graduated from the University of South Alabama where she met her husband, Tyler. Even though she is our newest team member, she is a major asset with her exceptional customer service and logistics experience. Emily lives in Mobile with her husband and they love spending time with family, watching all kinds of movies, and finding ways to travel on a budget.

Micah Moseley

Delivery Driver

Micah graduated in 2021 from College of the Ozarks with a degree in Business. During his college years, Micah served as a Firefighter and EMT. He took his experience as a first responder driver and has applied those skills to driving for STM. Micah always has a smile on his face, and is excited to complete the task at hand, no matter how big or small.
